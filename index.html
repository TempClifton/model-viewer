Testing the <model-viewer> tag.

Here's a picture of a tiki in Aero

  • Modeled with Signed Distance Fields
  • Processed with MeshLab
  • Textured with Substance Painter

And here's a 3D model of a tiki. Tap the button to view it in AR.

This is all it takes to embed the 3D model in a webpage: 

<script type="module" src="https://unpkg.com/@google/model-viewer/dist/model-viewer.min.js"></script>
<model-viewer src="models/Tiki.glb" alt="Stone Tiki" ar ar-modes="webxr scene-viewer quick-look" environment-image="neutral" auto-rotate camera-controls></model-viewer>
